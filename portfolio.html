Teen Dating Violence and Toxic Relationships

Check out my website on teen dating violence and abusive relationships! I built this website during the summer of 2021 at Girls Who Code through html, css, and javascript. I choose the topic of teen dating violence and toxic relationship because abusive behaviors, especially in teenage relationships, are not talked about or viewed as seriously as adult relationships.

Teen dating violence can happen to anyone and people need to be able to recognize the warning signs.
